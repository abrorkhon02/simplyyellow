Our Story

About me

For more than 15 years, I’ve lived and worked in the hospitality industry – always with a clear focus on coffee.

Coffee is more than just a drink to me – it connects people. Whether it’s an energy boost at a trade fair, a little break during the workday, or a moment of pure enjoyment – every cup matters.

I value regional quality: our coffee is roasted in Berlin, and our catering and desserts come from our family business, Zuka – fresh, honest, and made with love. That’s how we keep not only the taste authentic but also the values behind it.

Bae Le Duy

Barista with a mission: to make every cup a moment of connection and energy.
