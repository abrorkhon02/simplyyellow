Our Story

About Simply Yellow

For over 15 years, founder BEA Le Duy has lived in hospitality always focused on exceptional coffee. Simply Yellow represents curated coffee experiences, regional quality, and authentic service.

Our coffee is roasted fresh in Berlin, while pastries come from sister company Zuka. Every cup and plate connects people at trade fairs, offices, and events.

"Every cup should be a moment of connection and energy."

— BEA Le Duy, Founder
